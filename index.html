Research activities

I am currently an associate professor at Université Paris-Est Créteil where I teach computer science in the Sciences and Technology Faculty as well as the Health Faculty. I do research on guaranteed simulation and verification of cyber-physical systems at the LACL laboratory.

Formerly, I was a post doctoral researcher at ENSTA Paris, in the Hybrid Systems Semantics (SSH) group of the Computer Science and Systems Engineering Laboratory (U2IS). I worked on guaranteed co-simulation of hybrid dynamical systems, under the supervision of Alexandre Chapoutot and Julien Alexandre dit Sandretto.

Before that, I was a post doctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science of Aalborg University, in the Distributed, Embedded and Intelligent Systems unit (DEIS) under the supervision of Kim G. Larsen. I work on guaranteed control synthesis for control systems using timed automata abstractions.

Away back, I worked as a PhD student at CMLA, ENS Paris-Saclay under the supervision of Florian de Vuyst, Ludovic Chamoin, and Laurent Fribourg. [manuscript] [slides]
English title: Guaranteed Control Synthesis for Switched Space-Time Dynamical Systems
French title: Synthèse de Contrôle Garanti pour des Systèmes Dynamiques Spatio-Temporels à Commutation

Before that, I was a student at the Ecole Normale Supérieure de Cachan (normalien), where I obtained a Master's degree in Structural Mechanics.

Main research fields:

  • Control of switched systems

  • Symbolic methods for control synthesis

  • Partial differential equations

  • Model order reduction

  • Distributed (composisitional) control synthesis

  • Guaranteed simulation of dynamical systems
